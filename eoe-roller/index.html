Edge of the Empire Dice Roller

How-To:

Click the dice you want to roll. Every time you click a die, you add one of that die to the dice pool. Once you've added enough (you can see what you've selected in the "TO ROLL" field), click "ROLL" to, well, roll your dice. You can see the raw face values of the dice in "RAW RESULT", and the net successes, advantages, and triumphs in "RESULT" (a negative value indicates failures, threats, or despairs). Triumphs and despairs have not been added to successes or failures, respectively.

You can click "RESET" to reset the dice pool to zero dice. You can click "SAVE" to save your dice pool for later use, and "LOAD" to load the saved dice and replace the current dice pool.
